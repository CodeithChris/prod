VICUÑA PRODUCTIONS

Vicuña Productions is the creative studio of Lawrence Productions, a film director & producer based in Los Angeles. Our work explores psychological thrillers through atmospheric storytelling that moves between suspense, mystery and mindful moments. Merging cinematography, sound design, and narrative, we offer unique visual stories across feature films, commercials, and digital content.

ABOUT THE FILM

A detective investigating a brutal murder is drawn into a twisted game of manipulation with a mysterious suspect who never speaks a word. As he delves deeper, he realizes silence can be more dangerous than he ever imagined.
